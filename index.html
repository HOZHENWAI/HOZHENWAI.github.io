About

Born in Antananarivo, Madagascar, I had the chance to get a scholarship from the French government. I chose to study mathematics, first because of my love for the subject. Algebra was my favorite course during my studies but I chose statistics for my master because of its wide range of application. Luckily for me, I live in a time period of great upheaval in AI. This quickly became one of my area of interest in part because of it's mathematical background and it's potential. I studied at the Université de Franche-Comté later Université de Bourgogne Franche-Comté after the fuse of both region. It is also at this university that I got my PhD in statistics partly on extreme value theory and machine learning.
